Biography
======
**Bo Li (李波)** obtained his B. Eng. and M. Eng. degree from Henan University (HENU) and Civil Aviation University of China (CAUC) in 2010 and 2013, respectively. And he obtained Ph.D. degree in department of Control Science and Engineering at the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) with specialization in controls in 2016. Currently, he is a Professor with Shanghai Maritime University (SMU), Shanghai, China. His research interests include the design and control of intelligent unmanned systems, fault tolerant control, control allocation of over-actuated system, intelligent control and optimization method, reinforcement learning, etc. He has published significantly on the subjects with 2 books and several technical papers, especially further more than 30 papers were published by IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems, AIAA Journal of Guidance, Control and Dynamics, Aerospace Science and Technology, Nonlinear Dynamics, ISA Transactions, International Journal of Robust and Nonlinear Control, etc. He has been actively involved in various technical professional societies, such as Guest editor of Transactions of the Institute of Measurement and Control (SCI Q3), editorial board member of Modern Intelligent Times, Complex Engineering Systems; IEEE member; a senior member of Chinese Institute of Command and Control, the professional committee of the intelligent control and systems; a member of Chinese Association of Automation, the professional committee of the guidance, navigation and control; a member of local organization committee and a session chair in the 2018 International Symposium on Sensing and Instrumentation in IoT Era (ISSI 2018), a technical program committee member in ISSI 2019, ISSI 2022, and several co-chairs in international conferences, etc.
